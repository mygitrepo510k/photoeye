Twelve original Lois Greenfield photographs from her collaborative work with dancers are presented here in this exquisite, hand bound book. Limited to 5 signed and numbered copies, the book is bound by Eddie Mui in emerald green linen cloth with a matching folio case. This volume also includes an additional signed silver photograph, printed on 8x10" paper, chosen from any image included in the book.
