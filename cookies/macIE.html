Here is an example of the Internet Explorer screen on your Macintosh:


Step1: Click on the Edit tab (upper left of your screen, circled in red above) and go to Preferences... (circled in red below.).


Step 2: Below is an example of the Preferences window. In the middle of the left Category navigation (circled in red) is the Receiving Files category. Click on the arrow to the left of Receiving Files, the arrow not the word.


Step 3: Below is an example with the Receiving Files arrow activated (turned down). Click on Cookies, and the screen to the right will change to the below screen, with Cookie Settings at the top. Circled in red is the dropdown box that needs to read Never Ask when receiving cookies.
Once you have done this, click the OK button, circled in green below.
