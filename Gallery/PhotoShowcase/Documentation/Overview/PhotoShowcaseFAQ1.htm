Overview

  • What is the Photographer’s Showcase?
    A juried online print exhibition and sales space at photoeye.com that showcases serious, fine-art photographers from throughout the world.

  • Who should apply?
    Photographers from all levels who want to exhibit and offer to sell their work on a highly visible fine-art photography website. The Showcase is looking for personal, artistic work that demonstrates a freshness of vision. Acceptance is based on the quality of work, not professional stature.

  • Why should artists consider an online venue for exhibiting and selling their work?
    The barriers for entry into a formal photography or art gallery are extremely high. Each time a gallery represents a new artist, a major commitment of time and money is made. As with most galleries, photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe can only handle the work of thirty or so artists due to space and time limitations. Now with the advent of the Internet, we are able to show work by many talented photographers, allowing their portfolios to be viewed by thousands of visitors each day. photo-eye Gallery receives requests to look at new work each day, the Photographer’s Showcase now provides a place for high quality work to be shown, making it easy for our staff to look at work by new photographers.

  • How will the Photographer’s Showcase be advertised?
    The Photographer’s Showcase will be promoted in a variety of ways, including advertising and press releases in national and local media. It will be marketed in the photo-eye Booklist, a mailorder catalogue that is sent quarterly to a worldwide audience. Whenever images from the Showcase are promoted in the Booklist, with the artist's prior approval, credit is given to the Showcase artist.
