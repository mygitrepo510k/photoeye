Eligibility and Acceptance Criteria

  • Who is eligible for the Photographer’s Showcase?
    Anyone of any age or nationality who is doing interesting work with photographic materials.

  • What kind of work is the Showcase Jury looking for?
    The Showcase is looking for quality artwork that utilizes photographic methods, either exclusively or in conjunction with other artistic processes. The successful portfolio is a cohesive body of personal, artistic work that demonstrates a freshness of vision. Commercial or stock photography should not be submitted.

  • What if I’ve only recently begun photographing? Is there a required amount of time that I need to have photographed before applying?
    We are interested in photographers who are serious about their work and who are creating fresh, original images. Whether you are new to the art of photography or a veteran, we look forward to seeing your work, and encourage you to apply.

  • Do I have to have gallery representation elsewhere before I may apply?
    Gallery representation or prior experience with galleries is not required. If you are new to exhibiting your work, we encourage you to apply.

  • Does my work have to be published in a book for me to apply?
    Not at all. However if a book has been published, please submit that information with your online application. It will help us better understand the scope of your work.

  • I’m going to be in Santa Fe soon. May I personally present work for review?
    Initially we will jury portfolios exclusivly online, beginning with photographers who requested information last year. At a future date, we may accept other kinds of applications.

  • If accepted, will my work be in any shows in the Santa Fe Gallery?
    Through the Showcase, other galleries, museums, magazines and publishers view your work. Our formal photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe will exhibit one Showcase artist each year. There is always the chance that other venues will want to handle your work and/or photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe may want to represent you.

  • If accepted, am I represented by photo-eye Gallery?
    The Photographer’s Showcase at photoeye.com has a highly visible presence as one of three distinct exhibition spaces. The other spaces include photo-eye Gallery Represented Artists and Magnum Photos Collectors Prints. If accepted, you will be an exhibiting photographer online only, on the Photographer’s Showcase at photoeye.com.

  • Is there a chance that I will become a represented artist at photo-eye Gallery?
    Currently, photo-eye Gallery Santa Fe, is not reviewing portfolios and is not taking on new artists. However, there will be at least one Showcase photographer invited to exhibit at photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe.

  • What is a Photographer’s Resumé?
    A Photographer’s Resumé is much like a work experience resume but is focused on your experience as a photographer/artist. Examples of Showcase Photographer's resumés may be viewed under the Bios link by many of the Showcase photographers.

  • Is the Showcase a non-exclusive agreement, meaning I can show work at other galleries?
    Yes, photo-eye wants you to be successful as an artist. Unlike many online venues, we do not require that you sign an exclusive contract. Thus you are free to choose other non-exclusive exhibition areas, giving your work even more visibility. Our contract allows artists to show their work in galleries around the country and on other Internet sites.
