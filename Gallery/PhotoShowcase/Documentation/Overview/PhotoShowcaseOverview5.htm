Overview
Over the past twenty-one years, photo-eye has become the premier source for fine-art photographic books and prints. Our mailorder catalogues are sent quarterly to a worldwide audience and include the largest selection of art photography books available anywhere. photoeye.com is the web's foremost e-commerce site devoted to fine-art photography. It includes our 10,000+ book title database - all keyword searchable, and our online Gallery of represented artists.

photo-eye Gallery, Santa Fe, represents the work of thirty or so artists. Now with the advent of the Internet, photo-eye is able to show work by many serious, fine-art photographers from throughout the world. The Photographer’s Showcase allows photographers to participate by uploading and describing their portfolios online, then showing their work to photo-eye's great audience. photo-eye's demographics include curators, gallery directors, collectors, teachers, and other lovers of fine-art photography - over two thousand visitors a day - just the right audience to view portfolios of your own work!

photo-eye invites you to submit work to be juried for this exciting new exhibition space. The Showcase is looking for quality artwork that utilizes photographic methods, either exclusively or in conjunction with other artistic processes. The successful portfolio is a cohesive body of work that demonstrates a freshness of vision with no clichés. photo-eye is interested in showing personal, artistic work, not commercial or stock photography. Acceptance is based on the quality of the work, not professional stature.

Here are the basics…

  • Apply Online
    It's simple, fast, inexpensive, and it's all online! It costs as little as $185 for one year to be a Showcase photographer.
    - Pay a $35 application fee for the first portfolio, $20 for each additional portfolio (if submitted at the same time).
    - Fill out the optional bio, artist and process statements, and title list online.
    - Upload your images in the form of ~200k jpegs.
    - Notify us by email when your application is complete.
  • Your Work is Juried
    photo-eye juries your work and selects 10-20 images from each portfolio to be shown online.
  • Your Portfolios are Setup
    Sign the Artist Agreement and pay the setup and exhibition fees. photo-eye will then give you a login password so that you may access your portfolio setup pages. Fill out your artist résumé, chronology, artist statement online; sequence your images and fill out descriptive information about each print, all online.
  • Your Portfolios are Shown
    photo-eye reviews your online portfolios and gives final approval. Once approved, the one year non-exclusive contract begins and your work is live on our site.
  • When Sales are Made
    photo-eye Gallery Santa Fe, acts as your agent, taking up to a 40% commission on any sales. When a sale is made either online or by phone, photo-eye orders the print. The artist ships the matted artwork to photo-eye in Santa Fe, NM, USA. All matting, insurance, and shipping fees to Santa Fe are absorbed by the artist. photo-eye approves the print quality and presentation, then sends the artwork to the buyer. The buyer has a 14 day satisfaction guarantee return privilege.
  • When Rights are Sold
    photo-eye acts as your agent negotiating any sales of rights on your behalf with your approval.
  • photoeye.com
    photo-eye attracts an international fine-art photography audience and develops, maintains and markets the site.
