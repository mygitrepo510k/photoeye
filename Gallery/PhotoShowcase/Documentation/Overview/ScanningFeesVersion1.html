S C A N N I N G    F E E S

The artist is responsible for having scans made of their work to Showcase specifications. Save all images as jpegs using a .jpg extension. The compression ratio, should be around 75%, and with the width and/or height at least 1,000 pixels (~ 14 inches) at 72 dpi (dots per inch). Each file size should be around 200k. Images should be named beginning with the word 'Image' followed by the number in the sequencing with a numbering system in the order you would like the juror's to view your work. Image1.jpg, Image2.jpg, Image3.jpg, etc.

Local sources such as Kinko's, AlphaGraphics, an image house, a typesetter, or a custom lab may be able to provide this service for you. Generally, today's flatbed scanners for quality web scans are so inexpensive that it is better to purchase one and learn the process.


Optionally photo-eye provides a scanning services with the following fees.

  • photo-eye provides a scanning service for slides (prints and negatives will not be accepted). This includes slides 35mm and 2-1/4 slides.


  • The artist is responsible for shipping charges both to and from photo-eye. The return shipping charges via first class mail is $5.


  • After your work is scanned, allow 5-7 business days before your images will be accessible to you online at the Photographer's Showcase.


  • An invoice will be included with the return shipment of your slides. images, and payment is expected upon the return of your slides.


  • Make check payable to photo-eye Photographer's Showcase. Once payment is received, you will be able to access your Showcase online.
