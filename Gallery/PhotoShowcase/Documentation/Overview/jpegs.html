What is a JPEG?
A JPEG (.jpg) is the standard image format for viewing photographic images on the web. The JPEG format supports 24-bit color and preserves the broad range and subtle variations in brightness and hue found in photographs and other continuous-toned images. JPEG is supported by most browsers. JPEG compresses file size by selectively discarding data. A higher quality setting results in less data being discarded, but the JPEG compression method can degrade sharp detail in an image, particularly in images containing type or vector art. (from the Adobe Photoshop Help menu)
