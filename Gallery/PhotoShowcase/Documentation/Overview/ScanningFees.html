S C A N N I N G    S E R V I C E S

The artist is responsible for having quality scans made of their work to Showcase specifications. Save all images as jpegs using a .jpg extension. Images should be named beginning with the word 'Image' followed by the number in the sequencing with a numbering system in the order you would like the juror's to view your work. Image1.jpg, Image2.jpg, Image3.jpg, etc. The compression ratio, should be around 75% (Medium or High Quality Setting), and with the width and/or height at least 1,000 pixels (~ 14 inches) at 72 dpi (dots per inch). Each actual compressed file size should be around 100-300k.

Local sources that offer scanning services may include AlphaGraphics, a digital lab, a typesetter, or a custom lab may be able to provide this service for you. Generally, today's flatbed scanners for quality web scans are so inexpensive that it is better to purchase one, learn the process, and keep the quality control in your hands.
