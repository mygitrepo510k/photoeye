F R E Q U E N T L Y    A S K E D    Q U E S T I O N S

Website Questions
  • Who visits photoeye.com?
    We have a high profile market including collectors, curators, gallery directors, historians, photographers, critics, and lovers of art photography. It is the heart of the fine-art photography community, worldwide.

  • Who else will be shown on photo-eye.com?
    As an integral part of photoeye.com, the Photographer’s Showcase puts your work alongside other contemporary photographers from around the world who are seen in the online photoeye.com Gallery and Magum Photos Collectors' Prints. You will be in great company, seen with well-known and emerging photographers who are serious about promoting their work.

  • Will the Photographer’s Showcase be organized by artist’s name, process, or subject?
    All of the above. Every photograph becomes part of a powerful image database. Clients will easily find and view your work based on descriptive keywords that you supply, insuring that your work is seen in the appropriate context. Your name and work will also be seen in various directories and future online group exhibitions. Based on this information, viewers will be able to locate a photographer by name, process, camera format, and subject.

  • Will my images have a copyright notice featured?
    Yes. A copyright notice is prominently displayed on each individual photographer’s page.

  • Is it possible for people to steal my images from the website?
    Information on the Internet, regardless of the site, including text and images, is easily copied. For your protection, copyrights are prominently displayed on the Photographer’s Showcase website.

  • Can I add more images to my Showcase once the exhibition is online?
    Artists are encouraged to submit multiple portfolios of their work. Whenever any new work is added to your site, you must go through the standard application process before it is exhibited on Photographer’s Showcase.

  • Can I add or change any text once the exhibition is online?
    Yes, whenever you want. Simply use your personal access passwords to make any text changes on your site. You manage your site, therefore if your print prices have gone up, or if you want to add biographical information, you make the changes anytime you want.

  • How do I get my work on the front page of the Photographer’s Showcase?
    The front page of the Photographer’s Showcase changes constantly.

  • Can I advertise on my website that my work is in the Photographer’s Showcase?
    Yes. You are welcome and encouraged to promote the fact that your work has been accepted in the Showcase.

  • Can I make a link from my website to my show at the Photographer’s Showcase?
    Yes. We encourage you to get as much visibility as possible.

  • Can I put a link from my Showcase to my personal website?
    No. photo-eye wants our clients to remain in our website during their visit.
