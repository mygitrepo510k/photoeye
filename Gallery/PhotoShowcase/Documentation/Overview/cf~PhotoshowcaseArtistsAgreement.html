Artist’s Agreement

Overview

 Photographer’s Showcase is a juried online exhibition and sales site for serious, fine art photographers from around the world. It is integral to our high traffic website photoeye.com. The application process includes a one-time, nonrefundable fee. If accepted and one agrees to exhibit, there will be an exhibition fee based on the number of images shown, which is determined jointly by the jurors and artist. The artist may re-apply at any time with new images.

Terms of Agreement

The initial agreement between the artist and photo-eye begins immediately from the date that both parties sign the contract. The contract ends upon completion of the one- year exhibition period, which begins on the date the artist’s showcase is publicly launched.

Exhibition Fees

See separate fee sheet.

Non-Exclusive Agreement

photo-eye wants the artist to be successful in their career. Therefore, photo-eye does not require an exclusive contract with Photographer’s Showcase. The artist is left free to choose other non-exclusive exhibition arenas, giving their work even more visibility.

Renewal of Agreement

45 days before the contract ends, both parties will have an opportunity to renew. If both parties agree, then a new one-year contract is signed.

Ownership of Images and Copyright

The artist is the sole and exclusive owner of the copyright of all images they create, and that are part of this agreement. photo-eye has the right to offer these copyrighted works for sale on Photographer’s Showcase.

Copyright Protection

The artist should understand that the presentation of his/her images on the Internet enables the public to download their images.

The artist, not photo-eye, may legally challenge any copyright infringements, and will assume responsibility for all financial costs. Consequentially, the artist would be the recipient of all proceeds gained from the pursuit of such infringements.

Communication with Artist

photo-eye will communicate with artists by e-mail, phone, or fax. Artists are asked to have a working e-mail address, voice mail, or fax number that is checked regularly.

Promotion

photoeye.coM is promoted in a variety of ways, which may include advertising in national and local media, issuing press releases, and visibility in the photo-eye mailorder catalog. photo-eye often uses images on photoeye.com in these promotions. The artist agrees that photo-eye may, without prior approval, use, copy, distribute, publish, and publicly display any Showcase images for the purpose of marketing and selling the artworks.

Referral credits

Artists who exhibit their work on the Photographers’s Showcase may receive up to $15 for each new artist referred to Showcase. Credits apply to any future Exhibition Fees.

Print Prices

The artist determines the retail price for their artwork, however it should remain consistent in all venues. When determining the price, the artist should keep in mind that all matting, insurance, and shipping fees to Santa Fe, NM, USA are absorbed by the artist. The minimum price for each artwork shown on the Photographer’s Showcase is US $250.

Print Sales

Photographer’s Showcase is an e-commerce site with opportunities for print sales. Your price, size, title, and medium will be clearly visible with each print shown online. Artists will receive 60% of the selling price, and photo-eye will retain a 40% commission. The artist is paid 45 days after the work is shipped to the buyer.

Special Sales Discount

Occasionally discounts on prints are given to museum, other galleries, interior designers, collectors, and other qualified parties. Discounts range between 5% - 20% and will be split evenly between the artist and photo-eye. For example, if a 10% discount is given to a museum, then photo-eye will absorb 5% and the artist 5%, resulting in a 35% commission instead of the usual 40%. No prior approval from the artist is needed in order to discount prints.

Print and Matting Specifications

All prints sold must be processed to typical archival standards. Prints must have an unblemished surface with careful spotting of all imperfections. All prints must be signed and dated, and professionally matted using museum or 100% rag board.

Shipping Artwork

photo-eye will inform customers that, in general, they can expect to receive their print within 2-3 weeks of placing an order. When a sale is made either online or by phone, photo-eye will send a purchase order to the artist by e-mail or fax. The artist ships the matted artwork to photo-eye in Santa Fe, NM, USA. photo-eye approves the print quality and presentation, then sends the artwork to the buyer.

Insurance

The artist is responsible for insuring their artwork from their address to photo-eye. Once the images are in the possession of photo-eye, they are fully insured for liability and shipping to the buyer.

Returns

photoeye.com will accept all returns under our satisfaction guarantee policy. All customers who notify us within 14 days from the date of shipment and return the artwork in the same condition, will receive a full refund less shipping. The artwork is then returned to the artist at photo-eye’s expense and no payment to the artist is made.
