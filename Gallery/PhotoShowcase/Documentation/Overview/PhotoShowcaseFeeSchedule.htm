The Exhibition Fee Schedule is for information purposes only. No payment is due until the artist’s work is accepted, the number of images shown is determined, and the Artist’s Agreement is signed. The contract exhibition period begins on the date the artist’s showcase is publicly launched.
  • Represented Artists
    If an artist’s work sells during the contract period, part or all of the following Exhibition Fees for that body of work will be waived during the next contract period.

    • $500-$1000 Total Selling Price of Prints Sold 50% waived
    • $1000+ Total Selling Price of Prints Sold 100% waived
  • Independent Artist
    Since the Independent Artist sells directly to clients, no fee waivers are granted as a result of print sales.

  • A limited number of Fee Waiver Grants will be given to those who show financial need. Click for more information.

  • The artist is responsible for:

    • Providing scans of their artwork that are ready to exhibit online.
    • Uploading their scans and filling out biographical and print description forms online.

  • The payment options are:

    If prepaying, the full amount will be due upon signing the formal agreement.

    If paying in installments (credit card only), the first payment will be charged immediately and subsequent installments will be automatically charged as follows:

    • 2nd payment upon public launch of your Showcase
    • 3rd payment on the first day of the 6th month of your contract
    • 4th payment on the first day of the 9th month of your contract
