K E Y    B E N E F I T S

  • Exposure
    Your work will be part of a high traffic, art photography website that is visited by an audience of curators, gallery directors, mainstream art photographers, collectors, and lovers of fine-art photography.


  • Association
    As an integral part of photoeye.com, the Photographer’s Showcase puts your work alongside other fine photographers from around the world, along with artists in our other online Galleries, photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe and Magnum Photos Collectors' Prints.


  • Potential Contacts
    Through the Showcase, other galleries, museums, magazines and publishers view your work. Our formal photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe will exhibit one Showcase artist each year. There is always the chance that other venues will want to handle your work.


  • World Class Website
    photoeye.com is recognized for the beauty of its design, ease of use, and power of its image database.


  • Exclusive Website
    If you are chosen as a Showcase Photographer, you will have a unique web address, www.photoeye.com/YourName. Also, you may use your Showcase address as part of your own website.


  • Non-Exclusive Agreement
    photo-eye wants you to be successful as an artist. Unlike many online venues, photoeye.com does not require that you sign an exclusive contract. You are free to choose other non-exclusive exhibition arenas, giving your work even more visibility.


  • Print Sales
    Photographer’s Showcase is an e-commerce site with opportunity for print sales. There are two different classifications of Showcase Artists, Represented and Independent.

    Represented Artists

    ● photo-eye acts as your agent in fielding queries, securing sales, and following-up on orders.

    ● Prints are not consigned. Instead they are ordered on a special order basis.

    ● photo-eye is responsible for collecting payment and providing customer service to clients.
    Independent Photographers

    ● The Photographer may sell and communicate directly with clients.

    ● The photographer ships prints directly to clients

    ● The photographer is responsible for collecting payment and providing customer service to clients.
  • Image Database
    Your work will be part of a powerful image database. Clients can easily find and view your work based on descriptive keywords that you supply, putting your work in the appropriate context. Your name and work will also be seen in various directories and future online group exhibitions.


  • Book Links
    With over 10,000 titles in our Bookstore database, photo-eye has the largest selection of in-print fine-art photography books in the world. There will be links from your online portfolio to any books containing your work that are listed in our database, and vice versa. Customers will be able to mix prints and books on their order.


  • Portfolio Management
    Unlike many other e-commerce sites, Photographer’s Showcase encourages artists to manage their portfolio online. For example, through sophisticated keyword associations and genre classifications, you determine the context in which your work is seen. The artist also sequences their images, enters biographical information and updates print price changes.
