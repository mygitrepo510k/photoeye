The payment options are:

If prepaying, the full amount will be due upon signing the formal agreement.

If paying in installments (credit card only), the first payment will be charged immediately and subsequent installments will be automatically charged as follows:

  • 2nd payment upon public launch of your Showcase

  • 4th payment on the first day of the 9th month of your contract
