Upon Acceptance Checklist

Congratulations, the images marked on your Title List have been accepted onto the Photographer’s Showcase! Here are the steps you will need to complete to get your portfolio launched on online.

- Review, sign, and send the Exhibition Agreement contract to Photo-Eye

- Review, sign, and send payment for the Exhibition Fees to Photo-Eye

Upon receipt of your signed Exhibition Agreement and Exhibition Fee payment, Photo-Eye will issue you a password by e-mail, which allows you to access your online Showcase(see Going Online Sheet). It’s easy! Follow simple step-by-step directions to complete your site.

- Make scans of each accepted image in the 5 required sizes and upload them (see Scan Specifications Sheet)

- Review the Help Building Showcase Sheet, if Photo-Eye is helping you build your Showcase

- Launch your site to the public after it is reviewed by Photo-Eye.

- Review our Guidelines for Prints, Presentation, and Shipping Standards Sheet for selling your prints online with Photographer’s Showcase.

Refer new photographers to Photographer’s Showcase and save money towards your next annual Exhibition Fee. For more details, see the Referral Credit Program Sheet.
