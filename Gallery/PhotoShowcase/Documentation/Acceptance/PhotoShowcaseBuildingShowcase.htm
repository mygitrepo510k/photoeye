Fees for Building Showcase

Photo-Eye can help build an artist’s Photographer’s Showcase if they are uncomfortable with computers and the Internet. Setup charges include the production of an online résumé that includes a biography, chronology, exhibitions and collections listings, bibliography, and other pertinent facts about your work. If you fill out these simple step-by-step forms online, then there are no additional fees throughout the year. Artists are responsible for having quality scans made of their work to our specifications (see Scan Specification Sheet).

However, if you would like photo-eye to complete the online forms for you and build your Showcase, the following fee(s) apply.
