S H O W C A S E    R E F E R R A L    C R E D I T S

You may receive up to $15 credit per photographer that you refer to us who applies. The referral program is a great way for artists to reduce their annual exhibition fee. There is no limit to the number of photographers that you may refer.

Here’s how it works…

  • $5 Application Credit
    If you refer a photographer to us, and they in turn apply, you will receive a $5 credit towards your next scheduled Showcase payment, provided the photographer includes your name on the Showcase Online Application Form.

  • $10 Acceptance Credit
    If that photographer is accepted into the Showcase, you will receive an additional $10 towards your next contract period, provided the photographer included your name on the Showcase Online ApplicationForm.

  • Unused Credits
    No refunds will be given for credits not used.

  • Forgotten "Referred by"
    No referral credits will be given after the fact if the photographer does not include your name in the space provided on the Showcase Online Application Form.

  • Payment by Installments
    Credits will be accumulated during the quarter and will be deducted from your next quarterly payment. If the credit amount is more than the payment amount, the credits will be deducted on your next Showcase payment. If you are at the end of your payment schedule, the credits will apply to your next contract, if renewed. No refunds will be given on unused credits.

  • Payment in Full
    Credits will be accumulated during the year and will be deducted from the next contract, if renewed. No refunds will be given on unused credits.
