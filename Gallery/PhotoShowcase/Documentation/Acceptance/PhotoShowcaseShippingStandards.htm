  • Turnaround
    photo-eye informs customers that, in general, they can expect to receive their print(s) within 2-4 weeks of placing an order. When a sale is made either online or by phone, photo-eye sends a purchase order to the artist by e-mail or fax. The artist ships the matted artwork to photo-eye in Santa Fe, NM, USA. photo-eye approves the print quality and presentation, then sends the artwork to the buyer. photo-eye reserves the right to refuse any print or mat that does not meet the Photographer's Showcase standards.

  • Leave of Absence
    If you are out-of-town for an extended period of time, you should communicate when you expect to be back with someone at the telephone number and/or e-mail that you have provided.

  • Quality Packing
    All prints must be packed to minimize the risk of damage while in transit. We highly recommend that you use the following materials:

    • Print Pad available from Air Float Systems, Inc. 800-445-2580 or buy online at www.airfloatsys.com

    In addition to the outside carton, your work should be placed in a plastic archival print bag to help prevent abrasion of the print surface. One source for such bags is:

    • Light Impressions, Inc., 800-828-6216 or visit there website at www.lightimpressionsdirect.com
  • Insurance
    Artists are responsible for insuring their work for replacement value. photo-eye is not liable for prints damaged or lost in transit from the artist to photo-eye.

  • Shipping Charges
    Prepay all shipping charges. photo-eye will not accept COD shipments.

  • Shipping Method (U.S. Photographers)
    UPS or FedEx are the recommended carriers. Generally ship via the least expensive option, unless there are special needs.

  • Shipping Method (Foreign Photographers)
    Work must be sent via Air to expedite delivery. Check with the carriers in your country for the best rates.
