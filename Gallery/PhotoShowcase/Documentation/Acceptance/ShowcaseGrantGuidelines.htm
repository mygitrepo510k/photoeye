The Photographer’s Showcase is proud to offer financial assistance to artists. Our grants waive the one-year online exhibition fee. One grant will be offered each month during the grand opening year of the Showcase. Artists whose work has been accepted by the jurors are eligible to apply for the fee-waiver grant. Eligibility for the grants is based on financial need. To apply, accepted artists must provide photo-eye with the following information.

  • Annual income from the past calendar year

  • A brief narrative (up to three paragraphs) that allows
        the artist to describe the reason for the financial need.

  • This form must be mailed to:
      photo-eye Photographer's Showcase
      Attn: Fee Waiver Grant Application
      376 Garcia Street, Suite A
      Santa Fe, NM 87501 USA

      No e-mail fee waiver applications will be accepted.
