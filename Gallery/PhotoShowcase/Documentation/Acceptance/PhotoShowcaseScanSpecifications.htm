The Showcase is a database driven site that must meet our strict requirements for size and resolution. Every accepted image shown on the Photographer’s Showcase will be seen in six different sizes online. The highest quality scans are made from original prints, second best are scans made from excellent slide reproductions, and the third choice for quality scans is from finely printed reproductions.
  • Save images using the following specifications:
    - Width and/or height to be at least 1,000 pixels (~ 14 inches)
    - 72 dpi (dots per inch)
    - .jpg (JPEG) format at a high compression ratio
      (around 75%, with each file size approximately 200k)



    • All images should be scanned ready to display. After your images, they will be resized based on Showcase requirements. There may be additional charges if your uploaded images need adjustments.

    Color correct and spot all images before uploading them to the photo-eye server. photo-eye reserves the right to refuse any scan that does not meet the Showcase standard. We will approve all scans prior to showing the work online. If you are interested in having photo-eye build your site, please contact us.

    Depending upon your modem speed, each image may take 2-10 minutes to upload to our server. There will be a Finished Uploading screen when the file transfers are complete.

    If you have any questions regarding your images or this procedure, please call 1-800-227-6941 and ask for Photographer's Showcase help. We will be happy to assist you.
