Going online

Photographer’s Showcase encourages artists to control the context in which their work is viewed. To ensure that the artwork is seen under the best conditions, please complete the following steps for going online.


Here’s how it works…

• Sign and date the one-year Exhibition Agreement

• Send the Agreement and your Exhibition Fee payment to:

photo-eye Books & Prints
Photographer’s Showcase
376 Garcia Street, Suite A
Santa Fe, NM 87501, USA

• Use your password to access and administer your Showcase. Upon receipt of your signed Agreement and payment, photo-eye will e-mail you a password with user instructions.

• Go online to www.photoeye.com/gallery/password.html to setup your Showcase. Login by using your e-mail address and password.

• Review the Setup Introduction Overview

• Complete Steps 1 – 8 of your online Biographical Information

• Make scans of each accepted image in the required size (see Scan Specifications form)

• Complete Steps 9 – 10 to upload and sequence your images

• Describe each image on your site with Steps 11 - 12

• View your prototype exhibition site with Step 13

 • Request Approval of your Showcase with Step 14
