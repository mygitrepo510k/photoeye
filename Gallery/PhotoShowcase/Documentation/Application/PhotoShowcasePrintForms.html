Application Checklist

ٱ Complete both crème colored Application forms and sign the second one

ٱ Include an artist/photographer résumé and/or artist statement (in English, no page limit). This is optional and it in no way affects the chances of the images being accepted.

ٱ Submit up to 20 images in one of the following formats:

  • Duplicate Slides/Transparencies. All must be numbered and labeled with your name, title, medium and slide direction and must correspond to your title list (see checkbox below). Slides should be presented in clear slide pages; no presentation boards please. All formats accepted. No originals.
  • JPG scans on a 100 mb Zip disk or CD-ROM, PC or Mac (200 kb maximum per image).
    File names must correspond with your title list (see checkbox below).

Third option for U.S. residents only:

  • Original prints, 16x20" maximum image size, 20x24" maximum mat size. All prints must be titled and correspond with your title list (see checkbox below).

ٱ Enclose 2 copies of a title list that documents all submitted images (or use the form provided). Tittle list is not required for online applications.

One copy for our records and if accepted one copy to be returned to you indicating our Showcase selections. Please number each submitted slide, scan or print and cross-reference by number on the title list. Include the title (if applicable), size, medium, and retail price. If you send a scan without a printout, rename each scan image1.jpg, image2.jpg, and so on.

ٱ Enclose Payment

ٱ Include return postage for your submission. Return postage is not required for online applications.

  • For slides and scans on zip disks, enclose a self-addressed, stamped appropriate envelope (stamps only, no metered postage will be accepted, as the postmark will be wrong. Submissions with insufficient return postage will be discarded.)
  • For original prints, you will be charged a $15 return UPS shipping fee (refer to Application Fee sheet), payable by check, money order or credit card. Prints will be returned in the packaging you provide. Please enclose a return shipping label. Please specify insurance amount, if any, and provide a UPS shipping address. We do not ship prints via US Mail. We can ship via FedEx if you provide us with your FedEx account number.
  • We are not liable for damaged or lost items.

ٱ Read this important information about the notification of jury’s decision

Your work will be reviewed and returned within 2-4 weeks of receipt. Everyone that applies will receive written notice of the jurors’ decision. If your work is not accepted, the decision letter will include general constructive feedback. Due to time constraints, there will be no other feedback beyond this written notice.
