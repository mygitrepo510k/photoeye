    photo-eye has received an enthusiastic response to the Photographer’s Showcase, and will be ready to receive additional submissions from prospective Showcase artists in the near future. Initially applications will be accepted exclusively online, beginning with photographers who requested Showcase information last year. Please email us if you would like to be notified when photo-eye is ready to resume jurying new work. Since artwork will be juried year-round, there is no time limit for submitting photographs, nor is there a limit to the number of photographers who may be represented in the Showcase. However work will be juried using submission deadlines announced throughout the year. New portfolios are published on the site on specified dates.
