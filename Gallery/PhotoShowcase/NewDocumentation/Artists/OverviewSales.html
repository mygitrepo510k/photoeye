The Photographer's Showcase offers opportunities for print sales. Buyers may purchase artwork using our secure online ordering system, by contacting photo-eye directly. photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe handles all pre-sales and post-sales customer service.

The artist receives 60% of the retail price on all non-discounted sales. Payment to the artist within 45 days of the print sale provided the artwork is not returned under the photo-eye 100% satisfaction guarantee policy. Artwork sold on the Photographer's Showcase must have a minimum retail price of $200 (U.S. Dollars). photo-eye requires artists to be consistent with their prices across all exhibition venues. Occasionally photo-eye offers small discounts to important collectors, other galleries, museums, and agencies. If this occurs, the discount will be split between the artist and photo-eye. At no time will the artist receive less than 50% of the retail price of their artwork. No images will be shown discounted on the site without the prior approval of the artist.
