photo-eye invites all serious photographers—established and newcomer alike—to submit portfolios of their images to be juried for this exciting new exhibition space. The Photographer's Showcase is looking for quality, personal fine-art photography that utilizes photographic methods and techniques, either exclusively or in conjunction with other artistic processes. Commercial or stock work is discouraged. The successful portfolio is a cohesive body of work that demonstrates a freshness of vision. Acceptance is based on the quality of the work, supporting materials, the expert opinions of our jurors, and not necessarily the photographer's professional stature.

The Photographer's Showcase jury includes staff members of photo-eye. photo-eye may, at various times, invite prominent figures from the fine-art photography community to participate in the jurying process.

If you are interested in applying to become a Showcase photographer, please proceed to the next section where you will find a checklist of items needed in order to make the application process as smooth as possible.
