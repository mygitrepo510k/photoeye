Artist Agreement and Payment

Artists will receive an e-mail stating the decision of the Photographer's Showcase jurors within 10 business days after their portfolio is submitted online. If some or all of the artwork is accepted, photo-eye will e-mail a copy of the Artist Agreement and Payment Form to the Artist.

The Artist Agreement is a one-year, non-exclusive contract to be signed by both you, the Artist, and photo-eye. photo-eye agrees to promote and sell your work online through Photographer's Showcase. The main points of the Artist Agreement are found throughout the Photographer's Showcase information pages.

The following checklist guides you through the completion of your Photographer's Showcase.


[  ] Read the entire Artist Agreement. Call us at 505-988-5152 if you have any questions.


[  ] Sign, initial, and date the Artist Agreement


[  ] Fax or send only Part 5 of the Artist Agreement, the Acceptance of Terms and Conditions, to:

photo-eye Books & Prints
c/o Photographer's Showcase
376 Garcia Street, Suite A
Santa Fe, NM 87501, USA

Fax 505.988.4487

[  ] Fax or send the Payment Form



Administering Your Portfolio Online

Upon receipt of the signed Artist Agreement and your Payment Form, photo-eye will format your accepted images into the various sizes needed for exhibition. No additional scans are needed. Your online administration account will be created. photo-eye will e-mail you a password which will enable you to login to administer your Showcase during the one year term of the Artist Agreement.


[  ] Login to administer your portfolio. The login link is located on the Galleries home page. Enter your e-mail address and password

[  ] Complete your online Biographical Information
(Steps 1 - 10)


[  ] Complete the description of each image and sequence your images. (Steps 11 - 12)


[  ] View and check your Showcase (Step 13)


[  ] Request Approval of your Photographer's Showcase (Step 14)

photo-eye completes a review of your Showcase within 10 business days after receipt of your Request for Approval. Should corrections or additions be needed, photo-eye will contact you. If your Showcase is approved, you will receive an e-mail notification of your Showcase opening.
