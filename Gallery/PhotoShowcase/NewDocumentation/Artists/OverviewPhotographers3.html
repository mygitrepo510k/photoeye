The Photographer's Showcase is a new, online gallery where serious, fine-art photography is exhibited and sold. The newest of several online galleries at photoeye.com, it combines the prestige of a juried exhibition with the ease of the internet, allowing artists to maintain their own biographical information and portfolios of images at a very affordable rate. It is a true showcase for the serious photographer.

Whether you are a successful or aspiring photographer or a collector looking for that next great artwork, Photographer's Showcase is bound to become your primary destination. For over twenty years, photo-eye has specialized in fine-art photographs and photography books. Through our retail bookstore, mailorder catalogues, and our formal photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe, we have developed a focused audience that relies on photo-eye as a resource for fine-art photography. With the advent of the Internet the resources at photo-eye have become even more accessible to an ever-growing worldwide audience.

photoeye.com is an established, highly active e-commerce site where museum curators, gallery directors, collectors, critics, and others who are excited about fine-art photography browse and purchase fine-art photography books and prints. With the addition of Photographer's Showcase comes a significant expansion of the resources that have made photo-eye and photoeye.com recognized and respected around the world.

You will find several exhibition areas at photoeye.com. They include the represented artists from our formal photo-eye Gallery in Santa Fe, Magnum Photos Collector's Prints, and the Photographer's Showcase.
