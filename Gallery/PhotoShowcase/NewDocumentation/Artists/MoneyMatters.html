  • The payment options are:

    If prepaying, the full amount will be due upon signing the formal agreement.

    The Artist may choose to pay Exhibition Fees in four (4) equal installments. A $10 service charge will apply for each payment including the initial payment. A credit card must be used and the installment option must be indicated initially. The first installment will be charged immediately after both parties sign this agreement. Subsequent payments will be charged on the first day of January, April, July, or October, until all payments are made. No prior authorization to charge the Artist's credit card is necessary.
