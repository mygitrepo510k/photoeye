O V E R V I E W

The Photographer's Showcase is a juried online exhibition space at photoeye.com where serious fine-art photography is exhibited and sold. A portfolio-based site, the Photographer's Showcase allows visitors to explore a number of images from a single body of work. Multiple portfolios can be created for other projects, keeping your work well organized and viewed in the appropriate context.

Portfolios on the Photographer's Showcase are self-administered, allowing artists to input and maintain their own biographical information and update their image data. The opening of new Showcase portfolios are accompanied by an announcement in our weekly email newsletter that is received by over twenty thousand photography enthusiasts worldwide. We also encourage Photographer's Showcase artists to keep us updated of career advancements, including prizes and awards, solo or group exhibitions and publication announcements for inclusion in the newsletter. For almost 10 years, the Photographer's Showcase has been an impeccable marketing tool for the serious photographer.

Unlike many similarly aimed websites, Photographer's Showcase artists benefit from association with the world-renown photo-eye name and are linked to our high-traffic website, which receives over seven thousand unique visitors everyday. photo-eye is an institution in the photo world, with an expansive catalogue, including out-of-print book auctions, photo-eye Magazine and vibrant galleries and has become a resource for curators, galleries, collectors, critics and photography enthusiasts from around the world.

Beyond partnership with photo-eye.com, Photographer's Showcase artists also benefit from the strength in association with other talented photographers. Our careful jurying process keeps our standards very high, which is reflected in the unique quality of the work represented on the Showcase. The Photographer's Showcase seeks to represent promising immerging or established photographers to help provide the exposure need to bring them to the next step in their careers.

Through exhibiting on the Photographer's Showcase, a number of photographers have been presented with fantastic opportunities for career advancement and the exposure of their work to a wider audience. These opportunities have included exhibitions, image licensing, magazine features, gallery representation, and of course, print sales. Some of these opportunities may come from photo-eye itself, including possible exhibitions in our physical gallery space in Santa Fe, NM, as well as inclusion in photo-eye Gallery's booth at art fairs. These opportunities do not arise for every photographer, but photo-eye Gallery, as well as others who frequently visit the Photographer's Showcase, are always scouting for photographers who show special potential. The Photographer's Showcase has proven to be a fantastic way to position yourself for these opportunities as part of a website that has become a resource for curators, galleries, collectors, critics and photography enthusiasts from around the world.
