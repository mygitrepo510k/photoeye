Overview
Welcome to Photographer's Showcase Online Application
Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher are required to use our Online Application.

Step 1 - Pay Application Fee
photo-eye's Photographer Showcase is a high-traffic, fee-based, juried Gallery at photoeye.com for serious, fine-art photographers. If your work is accepted, it will be shown in portfolio format on the world's foremost site for fine-art photographs and books. photoeye.com receives over 2,500 visitors daily, nearly 1 million visitors and 24 million hits yearly, the heart of the fine-art photographic community. It is the primary online fine-art photography venue for showing your work.

Application Fee
The introductory online application fee is a nominal $35 for the first portfolio and $20 for each additional portfolio submitted at the same time. The application fee is based on the number of portfolios submitted, not on how many images are in each portfolio. The maximum number of images per portfolio is 20.

Setup Charge
If separate portfolios are accepted, the setup charge is $50 for the first portfolio, $25 for each additional.

Exhibition Fee
The exhibition fee is $10 per image for the first 20 images shown, $5 for each additional image. The term of the artist's agreement is one year. If payment is by credit card, all exhibition fees may - at your option - be charged quarterly along with an additional $10 service charge.

Fee Payment
Payment of the Showcase Application fee is required before you can continue with the application process. Complete step one and submit your credit card online. If you prefer to pay by phone, complete the payment information in Step 1 by choosing the Alternate Payment's Credit Card on File option. Then call us toll-free 800-227-6941 and ask to pay the Showcase Application fee. Then complete the application order form. At that point you will have access to the additional steps of the application process. photo-eye will charge your credit card as soon as your web application payment form is processed.

Step 2 - Artist Résumé (optional)
Include your education, exhibitions, awards, and any additional information that indicates your commitment to photography. If you already have a bio saved in your computer, simply copy and paste the text from that document into the form provided. Don't be concerned about any formatting changes. At this point, photo-eye is only interested in the information.

Step 3 - Artist Statement (optional)
Include a short statement (three paragraphs or less) about your body of work and/or what you explore as an artist. If these images are a visual narrative, please give the jurors some background information. If you already have a artist statement in a word processing document, simply copy and paste that text into the form provided. Don't be concerned about any formatting changes. At this point, photo-eye is only interested in the information.

Step 4 - Process Statement (optional)
Describe the technical process(es) you use in your work. Include any camera or darkroom techniques that make your work distinctive. If you already have a process statement in a word processing document, simply copy and paste that text into the form provided. Don't be concerned about any formatting changes. At this point, photo-eye is only interested in the information.

Step 5 - Title List (optional)
Enter Titles for your Images. Include Process, Dimensions, and Retail Price.

Step 6 - Your Images
Instructions on how to prepare your images for our jurors, along with the form used for uploading your work to our server is provided in this step.

Step 7 - Completion
Send an e-mail that notifies us that you have completed all steps. That's all there is to it! Normally a decision is made within one week. You will receive an email with the jury's decision and instructions as to how to proceed.
