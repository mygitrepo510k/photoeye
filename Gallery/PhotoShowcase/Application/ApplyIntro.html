Please note that Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher are required to use these administration pages.

Step 1 - Setup: This form is general information concerning yourself (i.e.. Name, address, etc.). All fields with a red asterix (*) are required. There are also spaces available for you to fill out your photographic schooling, influential books, exhibitions, and your artist statement. All of this information will appear on your bio page exactly as it is entered here, so please be wary of spelling, punctuation, and capitalization. Once it is submitted, any changes that you wish to make will have to be done here at photo-eye and could incur an additional expense.

Step 2 - Default Artwork Description: To speed up data entry and reduce redundancy, this page will be used as the default information when no other information is included for each submitted piece of artwork. This information will be used as a template for all of your images unless you override it by filling out additional information unique to each piece of artwork submitted in Step 3. On this page include the basic information that will be used on most or all of your pictures. For example, if all of your images are gelatin-silver, include that information here under medium. There is no need to repeat this information on the pages describing each individual artwork if they are the same. There is a field in Step 3 to use the default page for all information which is a constant for your images.

Step 3 - Unique Artwork Description: Fill out Information that is unique to each individual piece of artwork. If the information is the same as the information on the Default Information Page then leave the field blank and choose "Use Default". Fill out a page for each piece of artwork that has been accepted. There are 2 "Submit" buttons on the bottom of this form, one to continue to the next step and the other to fill out another form for a unique piece of work. Please be sure to fill out as many forms as needed for each individual piece that has anything different from the information filled out in Step 2. By doing this, you will be able to control all of the information for each piece submitted to the Photographer's Showcase.

Step 4 - E-mail us Upon Completion: This page is very self-explanatory and is a basic conformation for photo-eye that you have competed all of the steps required in the previous forms. It informs us of the status of your images for the site as well as if there were any problems that we need to know about.


Thank you for taking the time to fill these forms out. They insure that all the information that you want on your site will be correct.
