What is the Photographer’s Showcase?

An online gallery at photoeye.com that showcases the work of fine-art photographers

Who this is for?

The emerging and mid-career artist who wants to promote their work in a mainstream online gallery to a focused market

Why does this exists?

The barrier to entry into formal photography or art galleries is extremely high. Naturally, commercial galleries have to make money. Each time a gallery takes on an artist, it is a major commitment of time and money. Photo-eye Gallery spends thousands of dollars for matting, framing, hanging a show with announcements printed and mailed, and an opening reception held, each time we mount an exhibit. It is impossible to do this on a yearly basis for more than a dozen artists. Yet, there is terrific work out in the world that deserves to be seen. Each day we receive requests to look at new work. Photographer’s Showcase now provides a place for this work to be shown.

Who visits our gallery online?

We have a high profile market including collectors, curators, gallery directors, historians, photographers, critics, and lovers of art photography. It is the heart of the fine-art photographic community, worldwide.

Why should I apply?

Exposure of your work to the above community is the key advantage. From here you could be signed up by a gallery (perhaps even our formal photo-eye Gallery), your work could be sold to a collector, contacts could be made for commercial assignments, or rights to your work could be sold for use in publications.

Will work be sold?

We will forward any interest in sales directly to you. If a work or rights are sold as a result of this interest, we would receive a 15% commission on the sale. However, it is between you and the buyer to negotiate your terms.

Wendy! How is quality maintained?

Not only do we review scans, but we look at matted photographs, your shipping techniques, and the quality of the work, before going live with your images online.

How do I guarantee satisfaction for the purchaser of my work?

You will agree to give the customer a 10 day satisfaction guarantee. If for any reason they do not like the work, they may return it to you for a full refund (less their shipping charges for sending the print back).

What if they want to pay with a credit card?

Accept only check or money orders.

Who else will be in the gallery?

Serious quality photographers who are serious about promoting their work
