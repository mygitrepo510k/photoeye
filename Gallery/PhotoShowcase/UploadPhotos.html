Upload Photos for Review

 

Step 1 -
Please keep the maximum size of your jpg files to 50K bytes each
Maximum size should be 144 height x 144 width.
Step 2 -
 Your jpg image files must be named exactly as follows: Image1.jpg Image2.jpg Image3.jpg etc.
Step 3 -
 Click on the browse buttons and locate the directory in which your JPG files exist on your hard disk.
Step 4 -
 Click the UPLOAD button when you've included all files.
Submit at least 10, but up to 20 images (recommended).
   
  Enter your first name and last Name
with no space in between. (Mixed case only, eg. BobSmith)
